    • Brief Q&A:
    Q: What is one thing that surprised you about teaching?
    A: When I was in graduate school, I was sure that I only wanted to teach Percussion to high school students. What surprised me is how quickly I grew to love teaching younger students and how much I’ve enjoyed teaching Orchestra.
    Q: What is your favorite thing to do when you're not teaching?
    A: If I have to pick just one, it would be woodworking. It’s the perfect balance of math, creativity, intuition, and physical ability. I was horrible when I started, but I kept practicing and now I’m pretty good at it. It’s also something I can go to if I’ve had a bad day or if I’m feeling really anxious about something.
